Earth & Life Science

Objectives:
  • Describe the structure of the Earth and explain how it supports the existence of life.
  • Explain how geologic processes (e.g., volcanic activity, plate tectonics) influence ecosystems and biodiversity.
  • Analyze the relationship between the Earth's systems (geosphere, hydrosphere, atmosphere, and biosphere) and living organisms.
  • Illustrate the impact of the water cycle and other biogeochemical cycles on living systems.
  • Evaluate how natural disasters affect living populations and how species adapt to environmental changes.
